About Our Unit

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders

Established as an integral part of the prestigious 4(O) CTC under the Odisha Directorate, SOA NCC Unit stands as a beacon of excellence in character development, leadership training, and national service.

Affiliated with Siksha 'O' Anusandhan University, we are committed to nurturing young minds into disciplined citizens and future leaders who embody the highest ideals of patriotism, integrity, and service.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get comprehensive answers to common questions about joining our prestigious NCC unit

1

Why should I join SOA NCC?

SOA NCC offers unparalleled opportunities for leadership development, character building, adventure training, and career advancement. Our cadets gain confidence, discipline, and skills that benefit them throughout their lives while serving the nation with pride and honor.

2

What are the comprehensive benefits of NCC?

Benefits include exceptional leadership training, thrilling adventure activities, priority consideration in defense services, government job advantages, personality development, national integration experiences, and lifelong bonds with like-minded individuals committed to excellence.

3

Is there any fee for joining NCC?

Absolutely no fees are required for joining NCC. The program is completely free, and cadets receive uniforms, comprehensive training, meals during camps, and participate in all activities without any financial burden.

4

What is the selection process?

Selection is based on academic performance, physical fitness assessment, and personal interview. Interested students apply during the university admission process and must clear basic fitness standards and demonstrate commitment to NCC values.

5

What exceptional activities and events are conducted?

We conduct precision parades, adventure training camps, community service initiatives, blood donation drives, environmental conservation projects, cultural programs, sports competitions, and participate in prestigious national-level camps and competitions.
